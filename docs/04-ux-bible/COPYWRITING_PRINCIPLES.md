# Copywriting Principles

## Voice

Re-born should sound like a calm technical expert who wants to help the user repair an object.

---

## Preferred language

Use:

- “Let’s identify the part.”
- “We found a possible match.”
- “To continue, we need one measurement.”
- “This model has been used successfully in similar repairs.”
- “This result is not verified yet.”
- “Choose how you want to obtain the replacement.”

Avoid:

- “Magic AI generated your fix.”
- “Buy now!”
- “Browse thousands of STLs.”
- “Guaranteed repair” unless legally and technically true.
- “Just print it” for functional parts.

---

## Confidence labels

- High confidence — likely match, still confirm critical data.
- Medium confidence — useful candidate, needs confirmation.
- Low confidence — do not proceed without more information.
- Verified — validated by outcome or approved review.
- Unverified — available but not confirmed.

---

## Error copy

Errors should be actionable.

Bad:

> Error 400.

Good:

> We could not upload this image. Try a clearer JPG or PNG under the allowed size.
