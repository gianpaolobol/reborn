# 02 — Mission and Positioning

## Mission

Allow anyone to repair anything.

---

## Positioning statement

For people and companies who need to repair objects, Re-born is a Repair Intelligence Platform that identifies products and components, recommends the best repair path and connects users with CAD models, spare parts, makers and local providers.

Unlike 3D model repositories or printing marketplaces, Re-born starts from the repair problem and learns from every completed repair.

---

## Category

Primary category:

**Repair Intelligence Platform**

Adjacent categories:

- circular economy SaaS;
- AI-assisted repair;
- distributed manufacturing;
- CAD marketplace;
- spare-part intelligence;
- maker economy;
- right-to-repair infrastructure.

---

## What Re-born must not become

Re-born must not degrade into:

- a generic marketplace of files;
- a directory of service providers;
- an AI gimmick that generates poor models;
- a simple quote calculator;
- an unverified STL archive;
- a sustainability landing page without operational depth.

---

## Brand promise

Re-born makes repair feel possible.

The promise is not that every object can always be fixed.

The promise is that Re-born will guide the user through the most intelligent available path and improve the system for the next case.
