# 01 — Vision

## Long-term vision

Re-born will become the world reference for Repair Intelligence.

The platform will make repair accessible by connecting people, broken objects, AI, technical knowledge, makers, service providers and distributed production.

---

## Why now

Repair is becoming strategically important because of:

- growing replacement costs;
- sustainability pressure;
- right-to-repair culture;
- affordable 3D printing;
- AI recognition and generation;
- distributed maker communities;
- enterprise interest in circular economy;
- fragmentation of spare-part knowledge.

Re-born does not need to invent repair. It needs to organize, guide and scale it.

---

## The future state

In the mature platform, a user can upload a photo of a broken component and receive:

- identification of the object;
- likely component name;
- compatible spare part options;
- existing CAD models;
- confidence score;
- repair instructions;
- local production options;
- price and lead time;
- sustainability impact;
- post-repair validation.

The system becomes better after each repair.

---

## Strategic moat

The long-term moat is not the AI model alone.

The moat is the combination of:

- repair-specific Knowledge Graph;
- verified repair outcomes;
- maker/provider network;
- trust and reputation data;
- accumulated compatibility data;
- enterprise relationships;
- repair journey UX;
- wallet and incentive infrastructure.
