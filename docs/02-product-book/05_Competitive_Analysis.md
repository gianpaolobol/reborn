# 05 — Competitive Analysis

## Competitive frame

Re-born competes indirectly with several categories, but should not copy their mental model.

---

## 3D model repositories

Examples:

- Thingiverse;
- Printables;
- MakerWorld.

### Strengths

- large model libraries;
- active maker communities;
- easy discovery for known models.

### Weaknesses

- users must know what to search;
- models are not structured around repair outcomes;
- low compatibility certainty;
- limited repair journey guidance;
- no deep provider fulfilment layer.

### Re-born differentiation

Re-born starts from the broken object and uses knowledge, AI and repair outcomes to guide discovery.

---

## Distributed production marketplaces

Examples:

- Hubs-style networks;
- local 3D printing directories;
- professional service portals.

### Strengths

- production capacity;
- pricing and fulfilment;
- business-oriented workflow.

### Weaknesses

- assume the customer already has a manufacturable file;
- weak repair intelligence;
- limited maker royalty layer;
- no object-level learning.

### Re-born differentiation

Re-born helps create or find the solution before routing to production.

---

## Spare-part ecommerce

### Strengths

- direct replacement;
- familiar checkout;
- manufacturer or compatible parts.

### Weaknesses

- missing obsolete parts;
- weak identification;
- fragmented catalogues;
- no CAD fallback;
- no local production option.

### Re-born differentiation

Re-born can combine existing spare parts, compatible CAD, AI generation and provider production.

---

## AI 3D generation tools

Examples:

- image-to-3D tools;
- text-to-3D tools;
- reconstruction tools.

### Strengths

- rapid generation;
- visual experimentation;
- creative workflows.

### Weaknesses

- often not dimensionally reliable;
- not repair-specific;
- weak fit validation;
- no marketplace/provider/trust integration.

### Re-born differentiation

AI generation is one possible repair path, not the whole product. Generated models must be validated, versioned and connected to repair outcomes.

---

## Strategic positioning map

Re-born should own the quadrant:

**High repair intelligence + high fulfilment capability**

Most alternatives are either:

- high content, low intelligence;
- high production, low diagnosis;
- high AI novelty, low repair reliability;
- high commerce, low learning.
