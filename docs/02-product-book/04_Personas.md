# 04 — Personas

## Persona 1 — Everyday Repair User

### Profile

A non-technical user with a broken household object, appliance accessory, bike part, furniture component, toy, tool or plastic piece.

### Goal

Make the object work again without understanding CAD, slicers or materials.

### Pain points

- does not know the component name;
- cannot find the spare part;
- does not know if 3D printing is suitable;
- fears wasting time and money;
- does not know who can produce the part.

### Product need

A guided repair path with simple language, confidence indicators and local fulfilment options.

---

## Persona 2 — Maker / CAD Creator

### Profile

A user who models replacement parts and wants recognition, income and real-world impact.

### Goal

Upload useful models and earn from downloads, prints, bounties and verified repairs.

### Pain points

- hard to monetize useful niche models;
- existing model platforms reward popularity more than repair usefulness;
- no structured link between model and repair outcome;
- weak royalty systems.

### Product need

Maker dashboard, royalties, bounties, versioning, repair impact metrics and verified model reputation.

---

## Persona 3 — Independent 3D Printing Provider

### Profile

A single operator with modern desktop printers capable of producing good parts locally.

### Goal

Receive qualified orders without building an entire sales funnel.

### Pain points

- inconsistent demand;
- difficult customer communication;
- unclear specifications;
- price pressure;
- no trust layer against larger services.

### Product need

Provider profile, quote flow, capacity management, quality badges, payment protection and local ranking.

---

## Persona 4 — Professional Service Provider

### Profile

A structured 3D printing, prototyping or repair service.

### Goal

Acquire repair-related jobs and enterprise demand.

### Pain points

- high customer acquisition cost;
- many unqualified requests;
- need for reliable technical information;
- competition with low-cost operators.

### Product need

Qualified leads, SLA options, enterprise routing, professional badges and production workflow tools.

---

## Persona 5 — Company / Enterprise

### Profile

A company managing equipment, products, spare parts, customer repairs, circular economy or maintenance.

### Goal

Reduce waste, support repair, lower replacement costs and collect repair intelligence.

### Pain points

- fragmented spare-part knowledge;
- lack of aftermarket repair data;
- sustainability reporting complexity;
- hard-to-source obsolete components.

### Product need

Enterprise Portal, repair case management, analytics, API, private knowledge graph and provider network.
