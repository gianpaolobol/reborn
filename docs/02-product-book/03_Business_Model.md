# 03 — Business Model

Re-born uses a multi-sided platform model.

The ecosystem includes:

- repair users;
- makers;
- professional providers;
- independent 3D printing operators;
- material suppliers;
- component suppliers;
- companies;
- API customers;
- public or sustainability partners.

---

## Revenue streams

### 1. Distributed printing marketplace commission

Re-born takes a percentage on every successful production order fulfilled by a provider.

Provider types:

- professional 3D printing service;
- independent maker with qualified equipment;
- local repair lab;
- future manufacturing partner.

### 2. CAD marketplace commission

Re-born takes a platform fee on paid CAD model downloads.

The maker receives a royalty.

### 3. Royalty automation

When a model created by a maker is used in repair or printing flows, royalties are automatically credited through wallet or Repair Credits.

### 4. AI Premium

Paid AI features can include:

- advanced object recognition;
- AI-assisted CAD generation;
- repair probability estimate;
- dimensional reconstruction;
- multi-image analysis;
- priority model refinement;
- enterprise batch analysis.

### 5. Maker PRO

Subscription for creators who want:

- higher visibility;
- advanced analytics;
- monetization dashboard;
- model versioning;
- bounty priority;
- portfolio page;
- verified maker badge.

### 6. Provider PRO

Subscription for production providers who want:

- better ranking;
- quote automation;
- capacity dashboard;
- lead management;
- SLA tools;
- quality badges;
- enterprise leads.

### 7. Enterprise Portal

Companies use Re-born to manage repair, spare parts, aftermarket, sustainability reporting and internal maintenance cases.

### 8. White Label

Re-born technology can be deployed for brands, retailers, manufacturers or repair networks.

### 9. API Economy

External systems can access recognition, repair path decisioning, model search, provider routing or sustainability metrics through API.

### 10. Marketplace Materials

Commission or referral fee on filament, resin, spare materials, fasteners, inserts and repair kits.

### 11. Marketplace Components

Commission or referral fee on compatible spare parts.

### 12. Wallet and Repair Credits

Repair Credits enable:

- AI usage;
- downloads;
- bounties;
- provider payments;
- maker rewards;
- promotional campaigns;
- enterprise allocations.

### 13. Bounty System

Users or companies can post a bounty when no repair model exists.

Makers earn rewards by solving the request.

---

## Monetization rule

A monetized feature must not damage trust.

Ranking cannot be purely pay-to-win. Paid visibility must be separated from verified repair quality.

---

## First monetization priorities

For MVP and early traction:

1. provider commission;
2. paid CAD downloads and royalties;
3. AI credits;
4. bounty system;
5. Provider PRO only after provider liquidity exists.
