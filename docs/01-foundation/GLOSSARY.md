# Re-born Glossary

## Repair Intelligence Platform

A platform that identifies repair problems, recommends repair paths, connects users with solutions and learns from outcomes.

## Repair Intelligence Engine™

The core intelligence system combining recognition, knowledge, decisioning, learning and trust.

## Repair Journey

The guided process that takes a user from a broken object to a repair outcome.

## Repair Case

A single repair attempt initiated by a user or company.

## Repair Path

A recommended solution route, such as download model, buy spare part, generate AI model, print locally, open bounty or contact provider.

## Repair DNA

The structured identity of a repair problem: product, component, damage, material, dimensions, compatibility, constraints and outcome.

## Knowledge Graph

The connected representation of products, components, models, repairs, makers, providers, materials, outcomes and compatibility.

## Maker

A user who creates or uploads CAD models, repair instructions or useful technical contributions.

## Provider

A person or company able to produce parts, usually via 3D printing or related manufacturing.

## Repair Credits

Internal credits used to pay for AI, downloads, bounties, provider services or platform features.

## Bounty

A reward request created when a needed model or solution does not yet exist.

## Trust Engine

The system that ranks reliability of models, makers, providers, repair paths and AI suggestions.
