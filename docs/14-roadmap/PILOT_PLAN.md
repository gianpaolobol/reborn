# Controlled Pilot Plan

## Pilot objective

Validate whether people with broken objects can complete the Re-born repair journey and whether providers/makers can respond to structured cases.

## Pilot size

- 10 to 30 repair users;
- 3 to 5 providers;
- 3 to 10 makers;
- 1 to 2 admin/operators.

## Pilot category

Plastic replacement parts for household objects and small appliances.

## Pilot success metrics

- 70% of users complete case submission;
- 80% of submitted cases can be classified manually;
- 50% of classified cases receive at least one repair path;
- 30% of routed cases receive provider/maker action;
- 10 real outcomes are recorded;
- at least 5 failed/partial repairs generate useful learning signals.

## Pilot questions

- Do users understand the journey?
- Do they upload useful photos?
- Which fields are confusing?
- Can providers quote from the structured data?
- Are makers interested in bounty/model contribution?
- Which categories are too risky or unclear?
- What data most improves classification?
