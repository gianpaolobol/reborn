## Decisions taken

- MVP is intelligence-first, not marketplace-first.
- MVP starts with plastic replacement parts for household objects/small appliances.
- Classification is manual-first but AI-ready.
- Architecture starts as a PHP 8.3 modular monolith.
- Frontend uses HTML5, CSS3 and vanilla JavaScript.
- No Bootstrap, no purchased templates, no heavy glassmorphism.
- Wallet, enterprise, full AI generation and logistics are out of MVP scope.

## Constraints

- Preserve the mission: Allow anyone to repair anything.
- The user does not search for STL; the user repairs an object.
- Every feature must strengthen at least one asset: Knowledge Graph, AI Learning, Community, Marketplace Liquidity, Enterprise Value, Sustainability Impact or Objects Saved.
- Avoid shortcuts that make Re-born look like a generic marketplace.
